About Me

From the Classroom
to the Cluster

I'm Elliot Ngosa — the educator and researcher behind Raising Scholars. Based in Lusaka, Zambia 🇿🇲, my work sits at the intersection of instructional quality, teacher development, and education systems strengthening in resource-constrained environments.

As a School Support Officer at PEAS (Providing Equality in African Schools), I work directly with secondary schools in the South-East Cluster, supporting headteachers and teachers to build high-performing school environments. Before this, I spent years in the classroom as an Art & Design Specialist, implementing the Cambridge curriculum — and learning as much as I taught.

I'm currently pursuing dual Master's degrees in Educational Research (TECH Global University) and Education Administration & Management (UNICAF University). Everything I write here is grounded in both lived field practice and rigorous academic inquiry.
